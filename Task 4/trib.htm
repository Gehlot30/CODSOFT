Tribute to Jonathan Amral

About Jonathan

  • Born on September 21, 2002, in Goa, India.
  • Former player of the teams like Entity Gaming and TSM-Entity.
  • Known for his aggressive play style and consistent fragging potential.

Achievements:

  • Highest individual kill count: In the 2020 PMIS semi-finals, Jonathan recorded 16 kills, second maximum in a BGMI tournament.
  • Valuable Player: Jonathan has been named MVP in several tournaments, including Skyesports Championship 3.0, Skyesports Grand Slam 2022, BGMI Masters Series, Red Bull M.E.O. Season 4, and PUBG Mobile Pro League - South Asia Season 1.
  • Tournament wins: Jonathan has won several BGMI tournaments, including LOCO: War of Glory Grand Finals, Ranbhoomi Season 1, and Skyesports Championship 3.0. Esports Game Changers Awards: Jonathan won the Esports Game Changers Awards.
  • Mobile Player of the Year Award: Jonathan came in second place in the 2022 Esports Awards.
  • BGMI representation: Jonathan was selected to represent India at the 2021 PUBG Mobile Global Championship.
  • BGMI awareness: Jonathan and other Indian streamers and content creators requested that BGMI ban cheaters from the platform.
